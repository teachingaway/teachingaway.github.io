    • 1603 Elements of a Plant Application [R-11.2013]

    37 C.F.R. 1.163 Specification and arrangement of application elements in a plant application.

    *****

    • (b) The elements of the plant application, if applicable, should appear in the following order:

      • (1) Plant application transmittal form.
      • (2) Fee transmittal form.
      • (3) Application data sheet (see § 1.76).
      • (4) Specification.
      • (5) Drawings (in duplicate).
      • (6) The inventor's oath or declaration (§ 1.162).

    *****

    An application for a plant patent consists of the same parts as other applications. For information pertaining to the inventor's oath or declaration, specification and claim, or drawings, see MPEP § 1604, 1605, or 1606, respectively.
