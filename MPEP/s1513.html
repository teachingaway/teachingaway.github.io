    • 1513    Miscellaneous [R-11.2013]

    With respect to copies of references being supplied to applicant in a design patent application, see MPEP ยง 707.05(a).

    Effective May 8, 1985, the Statutory Invention Registration (SIR), 35 U.S.C. 157, and 37 CFR 1.293-1.297 replaced the former Defensive Publication Program. The Statutory Invention Registration (SIR) Program applies to utility, plant, and design applications. Effective March 16, 2013, the provisions of 35 U.S.C. 157 were repealed.
